To Do List for New Graduate Students

Double check with your advisor about the appropriateness of the courses you are scheduled to take. Class schedules can be found at LionPath↗.

Check out the Student Assistance Center for Counseling Services↗ on Test Anxiety, Stress Reduction, Study Effectiveness, etc., as well as Disability Resource and Services↗. Also check out Diversity/Equity services↗ and Police and Safety Services↗.

Financial Aid↗

The Financial Aid Office is available to answer your questions concerning applications, criteria and deadline dates for Federal, State and institutional grants, loans and scholarships. The office also assists veterans with benefit concerns and problems. You may stop by the office, located in the Swatara Building, or call 717-948-6307.

Madlyn L. Hanes Library↗

Check out the Madlyn L. Hanes Library at Penn State Harrisburg.

ITS Support↗

All students who attend Orientation should automatically receive Access Accounts. ITS offers a clean, clear, step-by-step Getting Started Guide↗ for new students. Also visit ITS Help Desks↗ for useful information.

Access Account Information↗

If you attended Freshman Orientation or Transfer Orientation, you should already have your Penn State Access Account. You will need your Access Account ID to access your email as well as an account that allows you to access the internet. Most importantly, your Access Account ID will be used by the department. So get an Access Account even if you already have email and internet access from another source.

Penn State Wireless Setup↗

To connect your computers or mobile devices to the Penn State Wireless 2.0, follow these Wireless 2.0 instructions↗.

After your orientation, an account will be created for you on our Sun Lab machines. A number of your classes will have work done on the Sun Labs. It is to your advantage to familiarize yourself with the Sun Lab machines and its operating system (Linux).

The Sun labs are located in W-210 and W-210A Olmsted Building. The labs are open whenever the Olmsted building is open, i.e., 24 hours a day year round, except during certain holidays. Once your Sun Lab account is created upon your request, you can use your PSU ID card to get into the labs.
